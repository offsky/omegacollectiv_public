Zazow - Generative Art Playground

Prototyping UX/UI Design MVP Development Web Apps
What Is It?

Zazow allows you to easily create unique algorithmically generated artwork using a variety interactive tools. You can make fractals, spirographs, and other interesting images by adjusting the inputs that control how the computations are performed.

Problem Statement / Need

For artists and designers, generating new and original artwork can be challenging and time-consuming, especially if a large collection of related artwork is needed. Producing algorithmic art requires a lot of technical knowledge about math, computer science and color theory.

Solution

Zazow provides a quick and easy way for artists to create unique algorithmically generated artwork. They can use the site to experiment with different parameters and settings to create visually striking pieces. They can then use these creations as inspiration for their traditional artwork or as standalone digital art.

Designers can quickly generate patterns, textures, and backgrounds for their projects. They can use the site to create unique designs for websites, posters, advertisements, and other graphic design projects.

Our Role

We needed a tool like this to create background patterns and artwork for our clients, so we build this website from scratch and continue to run it on the side.

ValidBot - Website Validation and Testing

Product Research UX/UI Design Website Optimization
What Is It?

ValidBot is a tool for web developers to test their websites and make sure that they are following best practices for speed, security and utility.

Problem Statement / Need

Misconfigured websites may be broken for a segment of users. Unoptimized websites can be slow. Users get annoyed and abandon websites that are slow and have poor usability.

Solution

Website owners and developers can run their sites through ValidBot's gauntlet of 100+ tests to find errors and discover easy ways to fix them.

Additionally, they can stay on top of everything by proactively monitoring the website which will quickly catch any errors that creep in over time.

Our Role

We came up with the idea, built the website and operate it and use it every day to make sure the work we do for clients is top-notch and error free.

Cobbl - Share Memories, Collaborate and Display Group Photos

Prototyping React Native Expo Mobile Apps
What Is It?

Cobbl is a user-friendly app designed to collect, share, and display group photos in one convenient place: Cobbl boards. With intuitive features, users can easily organize, tag, and instantly share their cherished memories. Cobbl offers a more private sharing experience than traditional social platforms and ensures users maintain control over their content.

Problem Statement / Need

Traditional photo-sharing platforms lack privacy controls and can be cumbersome when organizing and sharing group photos. Users often struggle to maintain control over their content and collaborate with others.

Solution

Cobbl offers an intuitive and secure platform that allows users to easily organize, tag, and instantly share their cherished memories. With customizable privacy and sharing controls, users can collaborate on personalized boards and display their photos on various devices, ensuring a more private and efficient photo-sharing experience.

Our Role

Built the mobile frontend for Cobbl using React Native. This involved designing a seamless and user-friendly interface that focused on enhancing user experience. Integrated various APIs to ensure smooth and efficient communication between the frontend and backend, ultimately providing users with a reliable and responsive photo-sharing platform.

VVC Livestream - A Multi-site Live Streaming platform for Client Events

Prototyping Live Streaming MVP Development Brand Customization
What Is It?

A Live streaming platform made with a divi multi-site WordPress solution designed to live stream client events across multiple branded subsites. Each subsite can be duplicated and rebranded to include clients' partners while showcasing the same central live stream event.

Problem Statement / Need

Organizations often struggle to host and promote live events across multiple platforms, while maintaining consistent branding and providing relevant information for speakers and attendees. This can result in disjointed user experiences and difficulties in event management.

Solution

offers a streamlined, customizable solution that enables clients to host and promote live events across multiple branded subsites. This centralized approach ensures consistent branding and easy access to event information, such as guest speaker and host speaker details, as well as upcoming events.

Our Role

Built a multi-site WordPress platform with live streaming capabilities. This involved developing a flexible template that allows for easy duplication and rebranding of subsites, ensuring a seamless and consistent experience for both clients and their partners.
